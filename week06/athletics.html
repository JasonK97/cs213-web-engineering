The Nation's Best Soccer Program

We ain't saying that we win every game, or that we have the best fan turnout, but being based out of the Greater Seattle area we recruit players from all over the Northwest, which is notorious for producing some of the best soccer players around. We provide a fun environment where our players can improve their skills on the pitch, as well as find a place to relieve stress from their academic endeavors.

Almost Ranked Football Team

With great athletes coming around, we offer not the best football team around, but the most artistic at least. With a focus on art and technology, we can't rely on recruiting the best of the best as easily as we can for our soccer program. While we may not have a conference ranked football team, we do pride ourselves on providing a great environment to experience Division One sports every weekend, and the fact we have never been bottom of the seed.
